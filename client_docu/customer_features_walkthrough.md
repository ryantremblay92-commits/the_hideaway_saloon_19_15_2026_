### Highlights Included in the Walkthrough:

1. **The Lookbook (`/lookbook`)**: 
   - Displays the public gallery ready to receive portfolio images uploaded directly by the staff from their internal portal. The design maintains the high-end, premium aesthetic.

2. **VIP Loyalty Profile (`/profile`)**:
   - The user's dashboard now cleanly integrates with the new `loyalty_profiles` backend.
   - Showcases the user's current **Rank/Tier** (e.g., Bronze, Silver, Gold).
   - Displays available loyalty points and appointment history natively linked to their profile.

3. **Native Concierge Booking (`/book`)**:
   - A step-by-step, premium booking experience.
   - Highlights the embedded **Artisan Concierge**, allowing customers to initiate a chat with specific stylists or reception while selecting their services.
