# The Hideaway Digital Platform: Feature Summary

This document outlines the high-end features and technical capabilities currently implemented for **The Hideaway Hair & Beauty Salon** digital ecosystem.

---

## 1. Premium Brand Experience
*   **Hero Concierge Animation**: A bespoke, kinetic typography experience using lowercase and uppercase layering to create a high-end, "drawing" text effect.
*   **Golden Ratio Aesthetic**: UI designed with curated HSL colors (Gold, Ivory, Charcoal) and smooth glassmorphism effects to maintain a luxury salon feel.
*   **Responsive Atelier**: A fully fluid design optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile browsers.

## 2. Customer Concierge System
*   **Smart WhatsApp Integration**: A "Chat With Us" floating interface that allows customers to select a specific stylist or service.
*   **Real-time Lead Capture**: Every interaction is automatically logged in the backend before the chat opens, ensuring 100% data retention for follow-ups.
*   **Dynamic Stylist Selection**: Real-time visibility of which staff members are currently online and available to chat.

## 3. Secure Staff Interaction Hub (`/staff`)
A dedicated, internal-only portal designed for salon operational efficiency.
*   **PIN-Based Station Security**: Each team member has a unique 4-digit PIN for secure access to their specific station dashboard.
*   **Live Inquiry Feed**: Stylists receive instant, real-time notifications (visual + audio) whenever a customer selects them on the website.
*   **Station Pulse Analytics**:
    *   **Leads Today**: Real-time counter of customer inquiries received during the shift.
    *   **Availability Toggle**: A one-click "Go Online/Offline" switch that updates the staff's status across the entire public website instantly.
*   **Backstage Messaging**: A secure, internal-only team chat for stylists to coordinate with the receptionist (Rebecca) or each other without leaving their stations.

## 4. Service & Ritual Atelier
*   **Bespoke Service Mapping**: Dedicated high-performance pages for:
    *   Hair Mastery (Cuts & Styling)
    *   Bridal Couture
    *   Skin Alchemy
*   **Dynamic Routing**: SEO-optimized slugs for all service categories.

## 5. Technical Infrastructure
*   **Supabase Realtime Backbone**: All staff status changes, customer inquiries, and internal messages sync instantly across all devices.
*   **Database-Driven Team Management**: Centralized staff metadata (pins, WhatsApp numbers, roles) for easy salon management.
*   **Next.js High-Performance Core**: Built for speed, SEO, and smooth Framer Motion animations.
